Our Policies

Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 3-4 business days. Custom orders may take 5-7 business days. For expedited shipping please contact us to check availability. International shipping options are available with calculated costs at checkout.

You will receive tracking information as soon as your order ships. Please inspect your order upon receipt and contact us immediately if there are any issues.

Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy on standard orders; custom orders are considered final and cannot be returned. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Warranty Policy

Every item comes with a one-year warranty for defects in materials, stitching, or craftsmanship. For warranty claims, please contact us with photos of the issue.
