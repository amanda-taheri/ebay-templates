Important Information

This is a replacement seat cover — not a slip-on/pullover. You must remove your original seat cover before installation.

Depending on your model, some of our replacement seat covers come with an attached heating element. You may need to transfer the connector from the old cover to the new one. We're here to help if you need guidance.

Airbag components are not included and must be transferred from your original seat cover where applicable.

Foam & Frame are not included. The seat cover must be installed on your existing foam cushion.

Our seat covers use clipping systems that match OEM specifications to ensure a perfect fit. If your original cover used 180-degree plastic retaining clips (which hook onto seat frames and hold the cover securely in place), the new one will use the same type. Some clips may have a slightly different shape or design, but they will attach in the same way and function exactly as your original clips did.

To ensure universal compatibility, we do not pre-cut openings for buttons, headrests, or seatbelt inserts. These must be trimmed during installation to match your specific seat design. The process is straightforward and ensures a precise fit.

While professional installation is always recommended, many of our customers successfully install their seat covers on their own. If you'd like to attempt a DIY install, we're happy to provide General Installation Guidelines. The process may vary slightly depending on your vehicle model.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know this is the right model for my vehicle?

We’re happy to help! Just send us a message with a few photos of your seats and the VIN sticker. Our team will confirm the right match.

Can I upgrade from synthetic to genuine leather?

Yes! We offer genuine leather replacements for many models. Contact us to check availability for your vehicle.

Will the seat covers support all factory seat functions?

Yes — our covers are engineered to fit precisely with your vehicle’s factory features, including power adjustments, thigh supports, heated/cooled seats, and more.

Are the covers compatible with seat airbags?

Yes, but the airbag components must be transferred from your original seat cover during installation. For safety, we strongly recommend professional installation.

How are these covers different from my original ones?

Each seat cover is built to OEM specs — matching the original fit, feel, and appearance. We use top-tier materials for long-term durability and comfort.

Can you match my original seat color and stitching?

Yes! We source OEM-matched materials and may request photos of your seats and VIN sticker to ensure accuracy in color, stitching, and perforation.

Can I choose perforated or non-perforated material?

Absolutely. Some models offer both options. Send us photos and your VIN so we can confirm availability for your specific vehicle.

Do you offer different materials or colors?

Yes — we carry a wide range of materials including genuine leather, synthetic leather, cloth, and Alcantara. Reach out with your needs, and we’ll assist you directly.

Do you offer custom seat covers?

We do offer custom materials and colors for many vehicles. Please note that custom orders are non-returnable. Reach out to discuss what’s possible for your seats.

Do these covers include the foam cushion and seat frame?

No. Our covers are replacements for your original upholstery and must be installed over your existing foam cushion and seat frame. We do sell foam cushions for some models. Please contact us for more information.

Can I make the installation myself?

While we recommend installation by a professional auto upholsterer, many of our customers do install the seat covers themselves with great results! As long as you have some experience, the right tools and patience, you can do it. We also provide general install guidance upon request.

Where can I get them professionally installed?

Any experienced auto upholstery shop can install our seat covers. We recommend professional installation for the best fit and finish.

The clip seems reversed — is that normal?

Yes — in some cases, the clip must be rotated or folded inward (180°) to attach properly. This is standard for certain OEM designs.

Why are there no holes for headrests, seatbelts, or controls?

To ensure the most accurate fit, we do not pre-cut holes. These access points should be cut during installation to match your seat's layout.

The heating element connector is different — what should I do?

Our heating elements are universal with 2-wire connectors. Some OEM heaters have more wires — in this case, you may need to transfer the original connector. We’re here to help if needed.

How quickly can I receive my order? Is expedited shipping available?

Each seat cover is made-to-order at our factory in Houston, Texas. Please allow 3–4 business days for production. Standard shipping is 3–5 business days. Expedited options may be available upon request.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide through eBay’s Global Shipping Program — with full tracking and a 30-day return window.

My tracking number hasn’t updated. What’s going on?

Tracking activates once the carrier scans the package. If it hasn’t updated after a few days, feel free to contact us — we’ll help right away.

Do your seat covers come with a warranty?

Yes — all of our seat covers are backed by a 1-Year Manufacturer’s Warranty covering defects in material and workmanship.
