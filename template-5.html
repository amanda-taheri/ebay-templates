About Us

Our mission is simple, to deliver seat covers that perfectly match your vehicle's original interior, bringing back comfort, style, and value without the high price tag of dealership replacements.

We take pride in being a known and respected leader in the auto upholstery industry. For over a decade, we've dedicated ourselves to producing top-quality, factory-match replacement seat covers that combine craftsmanship, precision, and durability.

Based in the heart of Houston, Texas, every seat cover we produce is with top-quality materials that we proudly source domestically. By keeping everything here, from sourcing to production to shipping, we ensure faster delivery times for our customers.
